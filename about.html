It's time to build

We host hackathons at Startup Sauna (Finland) every two to three weeks. You can get information about upcoming hackathons by following Aaltoes' social media channels.

Attending an entire hackathon is the only way to get into the community.

The long-term goal is to create a tight-knit community for people who like to build stuff. And get 100k$ so we can buy our own Spot.

Build It started in early 24 and is a project under Aaltoes.
